BACKGROUND
A. The Client is of the opinion that the Contractor has the necessary qualifications, experience
and abilities to provide computer services to the Client.

and conditions set out in this Agreement.

IN CONSIDERATION OF the matters described above and of the mutual benefits and obligations
set forth in this Agreement, the receipt and sufficiency of which consideration is hereby
acknowledged, the Client and the Contractor (individually the "Party" and collectively the "Parties"
to this Agreement) agree as follows:
SERVICES PROVIDED
1. The Client hereby agrees to engage the Contractor to provide the Client with the following
computer services (the "Services"):
• The Contractor is being hired to develop, deliver, and maintain a comprehensive
desktop software application designed for HVAC systems. This application will
monitor environmental data from an indefinite number of sensors, including but not
limited to temperature, humidity, and pressure sensors. The software will provide
real-time data logging and monitoring capabilities, ensuring accurate and timely
information. Additionally, it will generate detailed analytical reports to support
decision-making and regulatory compliance. The application will also facilitate client
queries, enabling users to raise inquiries and issues directly to support staff for
prompt resolution. The Contractor will provide installation support, regular updates,
and ongoing maintenance services to ensure the software remains fully operational,
secure, and up-to-date with the latest features and technological advancements..
2. The Services will also include any other computer tasks which the Parties may agree on. The
Contractor hereby agrees to provide such Services to the Client.
